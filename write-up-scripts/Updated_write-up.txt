Echo — Send Money With Your Voice

Millions of migrant workers across Southeast Asia send money home every month, yet remittances remain slow, expensive, and confusing. Echo changes that with a single spoken sentence.

"Send mama 10,000 pesos every first of the month."

That's it. No forms. No fees. No waiting.

Echo is a voice-first remittance agent built on Morph — the consumer blockchain for everyday life. Users speak in their native language, mixing local tongues with English the way people actually talk, and Echo handles the rest. Our AI pipeline transcribes speech, parses intent, and executes stablecoin transfers on-chain through an authorized agent wallet. No seed phrases. No crypto knowledge needed.

What makes Echo uniquely possible on Morph is automation at scale. Sub-second finality and near-zero gas make recurring micro-remittances viable — weekly allowances, monthly padala, even conditional top-ups when a loved one's balance drops too low. Morph's decentralized sequencer network ensures payments fire reliably with no single point of failure, even while you sleep. Recipients claim funds with just an email.

We built Echo because remittances should work like a conversation, not a transaction. Morph's consumer-grade payment rails make that real.

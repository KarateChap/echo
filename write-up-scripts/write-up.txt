Echo — Send Money With Your Voice

Millions of migrant workers across Southeast Asia send money home every month, yet remittances remain slow, expensive, and confusing. Echo changes that with a single spoken sentence.

"Send mama 10,000 pesos every first of the month."

That's it. No forms. No fees. No waiting.

Echo is a voice-first remittance agent built on Morph. Users speak naturally in their native language — mixing local tongues with English the way people actually talk — and Echo handles the rest. Our AI pipeline transcribes speech, parses intent into structured commands, and executes token transfers on-chain through an authorized agent wallet. Recipients receive a claim link, sign up with one tap, and instantly access their funds.

What makes Echo powerful is automation. Users don't just send once — they set rules. Recurring transfers fire on schedule. Conditional top-ups trigger when a loved one's balance drops too low. An authorized agent executes transfers autonomously, so money moves even while you're asleep.

We built Echo because remittances shouldn't require financial literacy. They should work like a conversation. By combining voice AI, automation, and stablecoin rails on Morph, Echo makes cross-border payments as simple as talking to family.
